# Remove Feature - User Guide

## Quick Start

The remove feature allows you to hide conditions from the suggested conditions list without deleting them from the database.

## How to Use

### Removing a Condition

1. **Hover** over any condition card in the suggestions list
2. A **red X button** will appear in the top-right corner of the card
3. **Click** the X button to remove that condition from view
4. The condition will immediately disappear from the list

### Restoring Conditions

#### Option 1: Restore All
- Look for the **"Restore All"** button in the header (next to the Treatment button)
- Click it to restore all hidden conditions
- The button shows how many conditions are currently hidden

#### Option 2: Refresh Page
- Simply refresh your browser page
- All conditions will reappear (removals are not saved)

## Visual Indicators

### When Conditions Are Hidden

1. **Count Display**: Shows "(X hidden)" next to the condition count
   - Example: "Showing 8 of 15 conditions (7 hidden)"

2. **Restore Button**: Blue button appears in header
   - Shows: "Restore All (7)" where 7 is the number of hidden conditions

3. **All Hidden Message**: If you remove all conditions
   - Orange warning icon appears
   - Message: "All conditions hidden"
   - Prominent "Restore All Conditions" button

## Important Notes

✅ **Safe Operations**
- Removing a condition only hides it from view
- Database records are NOT affected
- Condition data remains intact
- You can always restore hidden conditions

✅ **Non-Destructive**
- No data is deleted
- No changes to your medical database
- Reversible at any time

✅ **Session-Based**
- Removals only last for the current session
- Refreshing the page restores all conditions
- No permanent changes

## Common Use Cases

### Case 1: Focus on Relevant Conditions
You have 20 suggested conditions but only 5 seem relevant:
1. Remove the 15 irrelevant conditions
2. Focus on the 5 that matter
3. Restore all if you want to reconsider

### Case 2: Clean Up After Review
After reviewing all suggestions:
1. Remove conditions you've ruled out
2. Keep only the most likely diagnoses visible
3. Easier to compare remaining options

### Case 3: Temporary Filtering
Want to temporarily hide certain conditions:
1. Remove them from view
2. Work with the filtered list
3. Restore when needed

## Tips

💡 **Tip 1**: Hover over the X button to see the tooltip confirming it won't delete from database

💡 **Tip 2**: Use the count indicator to track how many conditions you've hidden

💡 **Tip 3**: The Restore All button makes it easy to start fresh

💡 **Tip 4**: Removed conditions don't appear in the Treatment View either

## Troubleshooting

**Q: I removed a condition by accident. How do I get it back?**
A: Click the "Restore All" button in the header, or refresh the page.

**Q: Will removing conditions affect my database?**
A: No! Removals only affect the current view. Your database remains unchanged.

**Q: Can I remove just one condition and keep the rest?**
A: Yes! Each condition has its own remove button. Remove only what you want to hide.

**Q: What happens if I remove all conditions?**
A: You'll see a special message with instructions to restore them. Click "Restore All Conditions" to bring them back.

**Q: Do removals persist after I close the browser?**
A: No. Removals are temporary and reset when you refresh the page or close the browser.
