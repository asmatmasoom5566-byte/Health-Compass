## Key Observations

### What You See in Every Example:
✅ Age (demographic, non-identifying)  
✅ Sex (demographic, non-identifying)  
✅ Visit sequence (First, Second, Third, etc.)  
✅ Clinical complaints  
✅ Treatment details  
✅ Response status  

### What You NEVER See:
❌ Patient name  
❌ Register number  
❌ Visit dates  
❌ Timestamps  
❌ Initial diagnosis  
❌ Any other identifying information

## Formatting Features

### Visual Elements:
- **Box drawing characters** for professional appearance
- **Clear section headers** for easy reading
- **Consistent spacing** between visits
- **Bold visit labels** for quick scanning
- **Response badges** for outcome tracking

### Ordinal Numbering:
- Visits 1-10: Word format (FIRST, SECOND, THIRD, etc.)
- Visits 11+: Numeric format (Visit 11, Visit 12, etc.)
- Provides clear chronological sequence

### Professional Quality:
- Publication-ready formatting
- Suitable for academic presentations
- Clean and standardized
- Easy to read and reference

## Usage Guidelines

### Perfect For:
✅ Case presentations

✅ Research publications
